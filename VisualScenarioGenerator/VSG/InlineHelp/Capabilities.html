Introduction to Capabilities

A Capability is a Unit's potential to effect or influence another unit.  For example, a unit might be capable of destroying or repairing another unit.   The acts of destroying and repairing are called Capabilities.

Every capability has a range of effectiveness.  Effectiveness is defined in terms of intensity, or magnitude, and an optional probability.  Probability dictates the "probability" of a capability successfully executing.   

How to Define a Capability

1. Right click on the "Capabilities" entry on the Object Types tree to the left.

2. Select Create Capability.  Enter an Capability name in the resulting dialog.

3. Choose an initial value for the Engram.
