Introduction to Levels

Levels determine how an emitter will broadcast an Attribute value.  Levels are defined in terms of variance or probability.  

Variance is useful in simulating "noise" in detection.  Variance dictates the "signal strength" of the emitters broadcast.

Probability, on the other hand, dictates the likelyhood the Attribute's value will be broadcast at all.

How to Define a Level

1. Right click on the "Levels" entry on the Object Types tree to the left.

2. Select Create Level.  Enter an Level name in the resulting dialog.

3. Select Variance or Probability and enter a value for the magnitude of Variance or Probability.
