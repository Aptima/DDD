Introduction to Teams

A Team is defined as a grouping of Decision Makers. 

A Team, is defined in terms of the other teams it is hostile to.  A Team may be hostile to itself, as in the case of "friendly fire".   

Furthermore, a Team's hostility need not be symmetric.  For example, the "Northern Coalition" may be defined as hostile to the "Delian League", however the "Delian League" may not necessary be defined as hostile to the "Nothern Coalition".

A Decision Maker's team membership is defined in the Decision Maker Section.

How to Define a Team

1. Right click on the "Teams" entry on the Object Types tree to the left.

2. Select Create Team.  Enter a team name in the resulting dialog.

3. In the Opposing Teams checklist box, choose the Team(s) this team will be hostile to.
