Introduction to Species

A Species or Unit Type define what Units can be employed in a scenario.  A species has four basic characteristics.

  1. A base type ; Land, Sea, Air or another species.
  2. A Subplatform Capacity grid; Defines how many subplatforms of each species this species is allowed to have docked at any one time.
  3. A weapon flag; Indicates that the species is a weapon.
  4. A remove on destruction flag; Indicates that the species will be removed upon destruction.
  5. A collision radius ; essentially how big the species is.
  6. A Species can be launched by owner flag; Indicates that this species is able to be launched by its owner Decision Maker, regardless of who owns the parent platform who contains this species.
  7. The Decision Makers allowed to own this species list; Indicates which Decision Makers are allowed to own assets of this species type.

In addition to these four basic characteristics, a user can define Combo Vulnerabilities, Singleton Vulnerabilities and States.

How to Define a Species

1. Right click on the "Species Definition" entry on the Object Types tree to the left.

2. Select Create Species.  Enter an Species name in the resulting dialog.

3. Follow the onscreen instructions.
