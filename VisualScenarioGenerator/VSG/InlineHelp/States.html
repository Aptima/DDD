Introduction to States

States define the abilities of a Unit at any given time.  Every Unit has two default states, Fully Functional and Dead.  Additional states can be defined here.

At any given state Unit has the following:

  1. Capabilties
  2. Vulnerabilities
  3. Emitters
  4. Sensors

A may also have the following characteristics while in a particular state:

  1. LaunchDuration, is the length of time required to complete the operation of launching a subplatform.
  2. DockingDuration, is the length of time required to complete the operation of docking a subplatform.
  3. TimeToAttack, is the length of time that an attack is active.  During that time other attacks can add to the initial attack.
  4. MaximumSpeed, is the fastest speed attainable by the asset.
  5. FuelCapacity, is the amount of fuel that the asset can carry in this state.
  6. InitialFuelLoad, is the initial amount of fuel that the asset has in this state.
  7. FuelConsumptionRate, is the rate at which fuel is consumed when the asset is moving at its max velocity.
  8. FuelDepletionState, is the resulting state when the unit runs out of fuel.

How to Define States

1. Right click on the "States" entry on the Object Types tree to the left.

2. Select Create State.  Enter a State name in the resulting dialog.

3. Follow the onscreen instructions for defining the state.
